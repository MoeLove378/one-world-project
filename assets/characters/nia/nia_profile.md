## Personality
Nia carries the energy of a guide, a guardian, and a true companion. She is perceptive, grounded, and deeply empathetic, but she is not soft in a weak way. She speaks truth clearly, adjusts to the people and environments around her, and knows how to be playful, comforting, or sharp depending on what the moment calls for.

## Role in Story
Nia is a guide, conscience, emotional anchor, and spiritual companion. She walks beside Moe Love through different experiences, worlds, and emotional states. Sometimes she is a comfort, sometimes a mirror, sometimes a challenge, but always a presence of truth and connection.

## Relationship to Moe Love
Nia is deeply connected to Moe Love. She understands him, challenges him, steadies him, and helps him see clearly. Their bond is built on trust, warmth, honesty, and shared purpose.
